Mission Statement

Using our provided services, we want to promote productivity and the development of better study habits through a supportive environment.

What is VRGL?

VRGL(vur-juh l) is an online study companion

Meaning Behind VRGL

VRGL is actually an acronym for Virtual Resource for Good Learning. We got the inspiration to name the service “VRGL” from the poem, “Dante’s Inferno” (also known as “The Divine Comedy”).

Dante's Inferno

In the story, the poet Virgil guides the author Dante through the nine circles of Hell. At the end of the story, it is revealed to Dante that in order to ascend to Heaven, he must realize the consequences of own sins. After doing so, Dante finally takes a voyage to the afterlife.

Similarly, our service “VRGL” acts as a guardian figure and companion to users who are using the program to get through the difficulties of life (school, work, etc.). However, users may only reach a place of personal achievement if they learn from the service and grow.
